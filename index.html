Portfolio

Web App
A workout app, for you who needs inspiration to get started training. This app will help you put together different types of programs, to get high value of each training. Our purpose with this app is to make your experience as manageable and intuitive as possible.

After we finished our work with this app, I developed a deep understanding of how to create value for the user through pertinent functions and cool features. We made use of a platform called Firebase and with the help from an API , we were able to drag content from Firebase into our web app.
Ecooking
Ecooking is a skin care company who creates organic products. I developed this website for Ecooking's users, for them to get information and guidance about the products and what they are made of.

My focus with this website, was to create beautiful content that matches Ecooking's visual identity. This is expressed through many illustrations on the webste. At the time Ecooking was a very new company on the market and I had a great idea about illustrating their story in the logo. Ecooking’s CEO started by testing ingredients home in her kitchen, which lead to her success. Therefore, I made a logo in Illustrator, which represents a pot.
Chicago Roasthouse
We made a collaboration with the take-away restaurant Chicago Roasthouse. The purpose was to optimize their website and create a more manageable user journey.

One of our focus areas was to design a new visual identity filled with informing illustrations. Among other I created the infographic underneath the video on the front page. The purpose of this infographic is to show the users how quick and easy it is to order take-away at Chicago Roasthouse. Besides, we spend time on creating delicious content for the users, which is expressed in our video on the front page.
U-days & SoMe
In this project we developed a podwalk app for the future students. The purpose of this app is to help the user find the right education. This app will give the user information about each education on u-days, which hopefully will lead the user to a decision on what they want to study.

I designed a new logo in illustrator for the app, where I reused the blue and orange, which already was a part of u-days visual identity. In that way I create something recognizable to the user Social Media was also a main target, to brand u-days. I made an Instagram competition, which should create attention to u-days and get more people participate.
